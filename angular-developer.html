Position title

Angular-developer

Description

Our innovative and growing company is searching for experienced candidates for the position of Angular Developer. Thank you in advance for taking a look at the list of responsibilities and qualifications.

We look forward to reviewing your resume.

Skills Required
  • Excellent analytical and debugging skills
  • 2+ years of experience in developing dynamic websites
  • 2+ years of experience working with AngularJS
  • Familiarity with principles of UI design
  • Skilled in chosen frameworks/libraries like jQuery or Bootstrap
  • Javascript, HTML5 (web components, new multimedia, and semantic elements)
  • Design and develop highly scalable, resilient cloud microservices, and APIs
  • Develop and support the front-end part of a large web system
  • Prototype components
  • Create cutting-edge user interfaces
Responsibilities
  • Demonstrate technical leadership ability in Angular, HTML5, CSS, Docker, Node,
  • Bootstrap, Express, npm, and Communication with RESTful web services from JavaScript
  • Perform analysis of requirements and design
  • Work in an Agile team process, collaborating closely with fellow developers
  • Show drive and interest in learning new systems, applications, technologies
  • Supports and maintains existing websites, and web applications, troubleshooting issues as they arise and recommending improvements
  • Ability to provide basic server support as it relates to web development (IIS, DNS, permissions) and work with our networking team for more advanced server configuration/support
  • Participate in development tasks for cyclical business activities such as month end and year end close, business forecast cycles
  • Work on day-to-day assignments under direct supervision
  • Lead the design and build-out for all internal-facing applications, consuming Rest-based services
  • Architect and participate in creating the long-term architectural design for our new front-end product
  • Collaborate with Product Management, Engineering leadership, and other teams to create and launch product initiatives from concept to deployment, using modern technologies.
Soft Skills
  • Ability to work with team members
  • Self-starter and highly motivated
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to handle high-pressure and fast-paced situations
  • Ability to work with globally distributed teams
Educational Qualifications

Bachelor's and Master's Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Science & Technology, Information Technology.
